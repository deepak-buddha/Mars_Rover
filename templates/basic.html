WELCOME TO THE MARS ROVER CHALLENGE!

Here is a picture of a typical mars rover to brighten your day!

You have the following options to proceed

    /run.html - runs the data from mars.txt

    /(enter_coords_here) - dynamically enter your new rover coordinate to update the location. Eg: http://localhost:5000/13N23N would change the coordinates of rover from 1 3 N to 2 3 N. (Please use POST function through Postman or other API softwares to run it)

    /update.html - shows the final updated rover positions after entering new coordinates
